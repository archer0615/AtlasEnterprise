# Atlas Enterprise Specification
## Compliance Framework

Version: 1.0

## 1. Purpose
Provide governance for regulatory compliance, auditability, privacy, and operational controls.

## 2. Objectives
- Demonstrable compliance
- Immutable audit trail
- Privacy protection
- Standardized governance

## 3. Compliance Areas
- Data Privacy
- Financial Record Retention
- Security
- Audit
- Operational Governance
- Change Management

## 4. Control Categories
Preventive, Detective, Corrective, Compensating.

## 5. Compliance Lifecycle
Identify
→ Assess
→ Implement
→ Monitor
→ Audit
→ Improve

## 6. Required Controls
RBAC, MFA support, encryption, backups, logging, approval workflows, retention policies.

## 7. Evidence
Configuration history, audit logs, decision history, policy versions, approval records.

## 8. Reporting
Compliance dashboard, exceptions, overdue actions, risk trends.

## 9. Monitoring
Continuous compliance checks, scheduled reviews, automated alerts.

## 10. Integration
Audit, Data Retention, Backup & Recovery, Policy Management, Permission Framework.

## 11. KPIs
Compliance score, unresolved findings, audit success rate, control coverage.

## 12. Future
Automated regulatory mapping, AI compliance advisor, continuous control validation.
