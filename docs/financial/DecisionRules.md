# Decision Rules

## Market Crash
If investment assets drop 50%, first recommendation should be: add if cash flow allows.

## Home Upgrade Delay
If stock market drops 40% before home upgrade and home price does not drop, recommend delaying up to 3 years.

## Emergency Fund
Minimum emergency fund: 1 year of living expenses.

## AI Warning
Primary proactive warning: home upgrade plan may be delayed.

## Lifestyle Budget
Reserve 15% of annual available funds for quality-of-life spending.

## Decision Output
Always provide at least Option A and Option B comparison.
