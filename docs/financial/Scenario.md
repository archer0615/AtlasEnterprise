# Scenario Definitions

## Optimistic
Equity return high, home price moderate increase, interest rate stable/low.

## Base
Equity return long-term average, home price market average, income growth 1%–2%.

## Conservative
Equity return low, home price higher than expected, interest rate moderately higher.

## Stress
Equity market -40%, home price unchanged or slightly lower, interest rate 3.5% or above, home upgrade may be delayed.
