# Project Atlas / LifeOS

## Purpose
A personal Life Strategy Operating System focused on financial decision support.

It should answer:
- Can I upgrade my home in or after 2031?
- Should I sell the current home or keep it and buy a second property?
- How much spouse contribution is needed under each scenario?
- Can I buy a TWD 1.2M car without delaying the home upgrade?
- Can I retire between 60 and 65?
- What happens if stocks fall 40% before the home upgrade?

This is not a bookkeeping system or stock tracking app.
