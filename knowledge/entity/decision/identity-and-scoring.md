# Decision Identity and Scoring

## Purpose
This split document isolates Decision identity, properties, scoring fields, lifecycle state, validation, and business rules from the parent Decision Entity Specification.

## Source
- Parent specification: [Decision Entity Specification](../Decision.md)

## Identity
Decision represents a financial choice with type, category, status, reason, summary, priority, scores, references, assumptions, constraints, snapshots, explanation, approval fields, effective date, expiration date, and audit fields.

## Scoring
Scoring includes decision score, confidence score, risk score, benefit score, cost score, impact score, urgency, selected option, alternative options, and explainability reference.
