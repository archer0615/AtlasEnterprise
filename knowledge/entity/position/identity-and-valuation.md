# Position Identity and Valuation

## Purpose
This split document isolates Position identity, lifecycle, ownership, properties, semantics, quantity model, cost basis model, valuation reference model, validation, and business rules from the parent Position Entity Specification.

## Source
- Parent specification: [Position Entity Specification](../Position.md)

## Identity
Position represents a holding quantity and valuation relationship inside a portfolio while preserving asset, account, cost basis, and audit context.

## Valuation
Valuation behavior covers quantity, cost basis, current value, valuation reference, price source, stale valuation handling, and derived portfolio contribution.
