# Household Identity and Ownership

## Purpose
This split document isolates Household identity, ownership, lifecycle, relationships, navigation, properties, validation, and business rules from the parent Household Entity Specification.

## Source

## Identity
Household is the shared context for assets, liabilities, loans, goals, scenarios, decisions, recommendations, retirement planning, tax profile, cash flow, and insurance views.

## Ownership
Household scopes visibility and planning context while aggregate-specific modules own their own mutation behavior.
