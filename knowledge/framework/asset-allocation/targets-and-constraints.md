# Asset Allocation Targets and Constraints

## Purpose

This split document isolates target and constraint rules from the larger Asset Allocation Framework.

## Source

- Parent specification: [Asset Allocation Framework](../asset-allocation-framework.md)

## Target Rules

- Allocation targets must align with Investment Policy Statement constraints.
- Targets must preserve portfolio, goal, risk, and time horizon context.
- Target changes must be versioned when used for recommendations or scenarios.

## Constraint Rules

- Constraints should include liquidity, risk budget, concentration, tax, and transaction cost considerations.
- Constraint breaches must produce warning references.
- Constraints must be evaluated before rebalancing proposals.
