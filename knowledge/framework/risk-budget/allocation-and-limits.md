# Risk Budget Allocation and Limits

## Purpose

This split document isolates risk budget allocation and limit rules from the larger Risk Budget Framework.

## Source

- Parent specification: [Risk Budget Framework](../risk-budget-framework.md)

## Allocation Rules

- Risk budget allocation must account for goals, portfolios, and life stages.
- Allocations must remain explainable and comparable across competing goals.
- Risk budget use must be traceable to assumptions and scenario inputs.

## Limit Rules

- Risk limits represent maximum acceptable risk consumption.
- Concentration limits should prevent excessive exposure to a single asset, market, or goal.
- Limit changes must be versioned and auditable.
