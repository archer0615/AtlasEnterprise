# Notification Dispatch Trigger and Channels

## Purpose
This split document isolates notification dispatch triggers, recipient selection, channel selection, and delivery eligibility from the parent NotificationDispatchAutomation specification.

## Source
- Parent specification: [NotificationDispatchAutomation](../notification-dispatch-automation.md)

## Trigger Scope
Triggers include notification created, scheduled reminder, escalation, critical insight, decision event, recommendation event, report ready, and manual dispatch requests.

## Channels
Channels include in-app, email, SMS, push, and provider-specific delivery where configured.
