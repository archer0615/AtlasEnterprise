# Decision Review Automation Trigger and Scope

## Purpose
This split document isolates decision review automation triggers, eligibility, and input scope from the parent DecisionReviewAutomation specification.

## Source
- Parent specification: [DecisionReviewAutomation](../decision-review-automation.md)

## Trigger Scope
Triggers include scheduled review cadence, stale decision windows, outcome measurement due dates, major financial events, scenario changes, recommendation changes, and manual review requests.

## Eligibility
Eligibility preserves decision status, household scope, tenant scope, permission scope, source freshness, and idempotency.
