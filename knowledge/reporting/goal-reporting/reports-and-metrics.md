## Reporting Rules

- Goal progress, completion, health, risk, financial, forecast, review, recommendation, decision, portfolio, and scenario reports must identify their evidence source.
- Metrics, KPIs, forecast, risk, cash flow, budget, dependencies, and timeline sections must remain reproducible from snapshots.
- Scheduled and exported reports must preserve generation status, export status, and schedule cancellation evidence.
