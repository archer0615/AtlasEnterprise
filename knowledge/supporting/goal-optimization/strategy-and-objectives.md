# Goal Optimization Strategy and Objectives

## Purpose
This split document isolates optimization strategies, objective functions, constraints, and scoring behavior from the parent Goal Optimization specification.

## Source
- Parent specification: [Goal Optimization](../goal-optimization.md)

## Strategies
Strategies include time, financial, cash flow, priority, dependency, risk, portfolio, scenario, recommendation, decision, resource, milestone, sequence, and multi-goal optimization.

## Objectives
Objective functions include time efficiency, financial efficiency, cash flow feasibility, priority alignment, dependency clearance, risk reduction, portfolio fit, scenario advantage, recommendation value, decision readiness, and milestone reliability.
