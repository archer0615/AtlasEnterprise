# Financial Health Score Debt and Goal Readiness

## Purpose

This split document isolates debt pressure and goal readiness scoring inputs from the larger Financial Health Score specification.

## Source

- Parent specification: [Financial Health Score](../financial-health-score.md)

## Debt Inputs

- Debt-to-income pressure
- Mortgage and loan payment burden
- Refinancing and rate reset risk
- Early repayment liquidity impact

## Goal Readiness Inputs

- Goal funding progress
- Time horizon alignment
- Required monthly contribution
- Scenario stress sensitivity

## Dashboard Use

- Debt and goal readiness can be summarized in dashboard scenarios.
- Warnings should identify the constraint driving a score reduction.
- Recommendation outputs must keep explanation and warning references.
