# Decision Insight Categories and Rules

## Purpose
This split document isolates Decision Insight categories, detection rules, validation rules, business rules, and lifecycle state behavior from the parent Decision Insights specification.

## Source
- Parent specification: [Decision Insights](../decision-insights.md)

## Insight Categories
Insight categories include Decision Quality Insight, Decision Risk Insight, Decision Performance Insight, Decision Success Insight, Decision Failure Insight, Financial Insight, Cash Flow Insight, Portfolio Insight, Goal Alignment Insight, Scenario Insight, Recommendation Insight, Optimization Insight, Simulation Insight, Execution Insight, Governance Insight, Compliance Insight, Behavior Insight, Historical Insight, Forecast Insight, and Operational Insight.

## Insight Detection Rules
Detection includes Threshold Detection, Pattern Detection, Historical Comparison, Forecast Comparison, Peer Comparison, Scenario Comparison, Rule-based Detection, Event-driven Detection, Scheduled Detection, and Incremental Detection.

## Validation and Business Rules
Validation Rules and Business Rules ensure insights remain tenant-safe, permission-aware, auditable, reproducible, and bounded to existing Atlas Catalog concepts.

## State Machine
Insight state machine behavior records states, transitions, triggers, invariant checks, illegal transitions, operator, source, timestamp, reason, prior state, next state, and correlation identifier.
