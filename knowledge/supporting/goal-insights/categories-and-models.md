# Goal Insights Categories and Models

## Purpose
This split document isolates Goal Insight categories and model fields from the parent Goal Insights specification.

## Source
- Parent specification: [Goal Insights](../goal-insights.md)

## Category Scope
Goal Insight categories include progress, financial, cash flow, goal health, priority, dependency, milestone, delay, forecast, recommendation, decision, risk, behavior, portfolio, scenario, optimization, trend, and opportunity insights.

## Progress and Health Models
Progress, milestone, delay, and goal health models capture progress percent, completion score, health score, confidence score, schedule variance, expected completion, overdue state, dependency state, blocker evidence, and target-date proximity.

## Financial and Cash Flow Models
Financial, cash-flow, portfolio, and opportunity models capture funding gap, budget pressure, contribution capacity, surplus, deficit, liquidity, allocation, risk, valuation, performance evidence, period, currency, and masking state where authorized.

## Recommendation and Decision Models
Recommendation and decision models capture recommendation mapping, adoption state, expected impact, realized impact, DecisionSessionId, decision status, pending or stale decision signals, accepted or rejected decision impact, and completion evidence.

## Forecast, Scenario, Risk, and Optimization Models
Forecast, scenario, risk, and optimization models capture forecast movement, confidence change, baseline difference, scenario delta, assumption version, threshold crossing, risk trend, and better-path output from existing optimization results.

## Behavior and Trend Models
Behavior and trend models capture update cadence, review cadence, activity patterns where allowed, historical movement, rolling average change, sustained direction, and freshness of source evidence.
