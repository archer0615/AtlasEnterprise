# Decision Lifecycle Governance and Testing

## Purpose
This split document isolates decision lifecycle governance, permissions, security, performance, testing, edge cases, and consistency checks from the parent Decision Lifecycle specification.

## Source
- Parent specification: [Decision Lifecycle](../decision-lifecycle.md)

## Governance
Governance covers immutable history, permission-aware state changes, household isolation, retention, restore rules, and deleted-state protection.

## Testing
Testing covers state transitions, invalid transitions, acceptance, rejection, defer, execution, outcome measurement, archive, restore, concurrency, and edge cases.
