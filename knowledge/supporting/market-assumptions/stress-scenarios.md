# Market Assumptions Stress Scenarios

## Purpose

This split document isolates market stress assumptions from the larger Market Assumptions document.

## Source

- Parent specification: [Market Assumptions](../market-assumptions.md)

## Stress Rules

- Stress scenarios must define shock size, affected assets, and planning period.
- Stress assumptions must not silently override baseline assumptions.
- Stress results must include warning references when they affect recommendations.

## Dashboard Use

- Dashboard stress labels must remain separate from canonical assumption definitions.
- Stress outputs may summarize drawdown, reserve months, and goal funding risk.
- Scenario comparison must preserve baseline and stressed alternatives.
