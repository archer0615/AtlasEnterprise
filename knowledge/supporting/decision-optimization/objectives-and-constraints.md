# Decision Optimization Objectives and Constraints

## Purpose
This split document isolates Decision Optimization objectives, constraints, algorithms, and scoring behavior from the parent Decision Optimization specification.

## Source
- Parent specification: [Decision Optimization](../decision-optimization.md)

## Objectives
Objectives include financial return, cash flow stability, risk reduction, goal achievement, portfolio balance, liquidity, tax efficiency, debt optimization, time optimization, priority optimization, user preference alignment, execution feasibility, and business value.

## Constraints
Constraints include hard, soft, budget, cash flow, risk, goal, scenario, portfolio, execution, user preference, compliance, and regulatory constraints.

## Algorithms
Algorithms include weighted optimization, constraint satisfaction, multi-objective optimization, Pareto optimization, heuristic optimization, rule-based optimization, scenario optimization, incremental optimization, sensitivity analysis, and trade-off analysis.
