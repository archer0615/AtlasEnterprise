# Portfolio Holdings and Allocation

## Purpose

This split document isolates holdings and allocation concepts from the larger Portfolio knowledge base.

## Source

- Parent specification: [Portfolio Knowledge Base](../portfolio.md)

## Holdings Rules

- Holdings represent portfolio positions and must remain separate from Asset identity.
- Holdings must preserve quantity, valuation, currency, and source snapshot where applicable.
- Holdings are used for performance, risk, allocation, and scenario analysis.

## Allocation Rules

- Allocation compares current holdings with target policy.
- Allocation outputs may feed rebalancing, recommendation, and dashboard workflows.
- Allocation calculations must preserve assumptions and formula versions.
