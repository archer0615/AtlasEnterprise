# Goal Governance Policy and Compliance

## Purpose
This split document isolates Goal Governance policies, principles, compliance rules, and enforcement responsibilities from the parent Goal Governance specification.

## Source
- Parent specification: [Goal Governance](../goal-governance.md)

## Policy Scope
Goal governance policies cover goal validity, ownership, priority, evidence, review cadence, risk controls, compliance expectations, and exception handling.

## Compliance
Compliance checks preserve household isolation, permission-aware source access, audit evidence, policy version, and governance outcome.
