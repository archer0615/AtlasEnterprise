# Investment Policy Rules and Allocation

## Purpose
This split document isolates investment policy principles, investable universe, allocation policy, portfolio rules, rebalancing, and risk management from the parent Investment Policy Knowledge Base.

## Source
- Parent specification: [Investment Policy Knowledge Base](../investment-policy.md)

## Core Principles
Investment decisions must align with Blueprint, IPS, risk capacity, liquidity needs, and long-term life goals.

## Investment Universe
- Cash
- Fixed Income
- Equity
- Alternative

## Asset Allocation
Asset allocation defines strategic target weights, minimum and maximum ranges, and rebalancing expectations for each supported asset class.

## Portfolio Rules
Portfolio rules constrain holdings, concentration, liquidity, investment fit, and policy exceptions.

## Risk Management
Risk management covers risk capacity, drawdown tolerance, liquidity reserve, concentration control, and review triggers.
